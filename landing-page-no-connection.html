Frequently Asked Questions

I can't see the Hydrolink hotspot in my Wi-Fi list

Make sure your Hydrolink device is powered on and in setup mode. The LED should be blinking blue. If you still can't see it, try refreshing your Wi-Fi list or moving closer to the device.

What happens after I submit my Wi-Fi credentials?

Your device will attempt to connect to your Wi-Fi network. Once successful, you'll be automatically redirected to the main dashboard. This process usually takes 30-60 seconds.

My Wi-Fi password contains special characters. Will it work?

Yes, the system supports all standard Wi-Fi password characters including special symbols. Make sure to enter the password exactly as it appears, including capitalization.

The setup is taking too long. What should I do?

If the setup takes more than 2 minutes, check that your Wi-Fi credentials are correct and that your router is working properly. You can restart the setup process by power cycling your Hydrolink device.

Can I change the Wi-Fi network later?

Yes, you can reconfigure the Wi-Fi settings anytime through the device settings in the main dashboard, or by holding the reset button for 10 seconds to enter setup mode again.
