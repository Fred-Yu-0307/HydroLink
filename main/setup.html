Installation Instructions
Connect Device to Faucet

Attach the Hydrolink device directly to your water faucet using the provided connector. Ensure a tight, leak-proof connection.

Connect Hose to Water Drum

Run the water hose from the device output to your water storage drum. Make sure the hose reaches the bottom.

Place Ultrasonic Sensor

Position the ultrasonic sensor at the top of your water drum, pointing downward. Secure it firmly to prevent movement.

Scanning Information
How it works

The ultrasonic sensor measures from the top of the drum to the bottom to establish a baseline. This value is uploaded to the cloud.

  • Ensure the drum is completely empty.
  • Sensor must be positioned at the highest rim.
  • Keep the area clear to avoid sonar interference.
