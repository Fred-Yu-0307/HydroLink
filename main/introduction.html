Implementation

How HydroLink Operates

1

Install the Hardware

Plumb the smart valve onto your inlet pipe and clip the ultrasonic sensor module onto your storage drum's lid. Setup takes under 20 minutes without specialized tools.

2

Configure Mobile Rules

Pair HydroLink to your home Wi-Fi and set your maximum fill threshold (e.g., 90%) to leave safe room for water displacement and prevent sloshing.

3

Automatic Detection

When the municipality turns on the water at any hour, the inline pressure sensor detects it immediately and securely notifies your mobile device.

4

Precision Shutoff

The system opens the valve, monitors the rising surface level via sonar pulses, and shuts off exactly at your target. Zero spills, zero stress.
